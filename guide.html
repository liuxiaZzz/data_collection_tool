How to Use the Data Collection App

Step 1: Installation

1. Open Safari on your iPad

2. Add the app to your home screen for the best experience:

  • Tap the share button at the bottom of Safari
  • Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • Tap "Add" in the popup dialog

Step 2: Basic Usage

Adding New Records:

  • Initial is required, other fields can be filled later
  • Enter the patient's initial
  • Optionally fill in age, gender, and type
  • Click "Save" to store the record

Step 3: Managing Records

Searching Records:

  • Use the search box above the records list
  • Type the patient's initial to filter records
  • Matching text will be highlighted

Editing Records:

  • Click "Edit" on any record to modify it
  • Initial cannot be changed once saved
  • Other fields can be updated at any time

Incomplete Records:

  • Records with missing fields are highlighted in red
  • Missing fields are listed below each record
  • All fields must be completed before exporting

Step 4: Exporting Data

  • Click "Export Data" to download records as CSV
  • All records must be complete before exporting
  • Export includes: Initial, Age, Gender, Type, and Timestamp
  • Regular exports are recommended for backup

Important Notes:

  • The app works completely offline
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Regular data exports are recommended for backup
  • The app will automatically update when online

Troubleshooting:

Issue 1: Can't find "Add to Home Screen"?
Make sure you're using Safari browser, as other browsers may not support this feature.

Issue 2: App not working offline?
Ensure you've opened the app at least once while online to cache all necessary files.

Issue 3: Data not saving?
Make sure you're not using Safari's private browsing mode and have sufficient storage space.

Technical Support:

If you encounter issues, please check:

  • Safari is updated to the latest version
  • Necessary permissions are granted
  • Sufficient device storage is available
  • You're not in private browsing mode
